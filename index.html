03 — Process

Crisp, fast, no theater.

Most engagements run 4–8 weeks. We over-communicate in writing, ship every Friday, and keep a single shared dashboard so you always know where things stand.

  1. 01

    Mint

    Half-day strategy intensive. We map the audience, the offer, and the smallest thing that would move the needle. You leave with a one-page brief and a fixed quote.

  2. 02

    Make

    Design and engineering run in parallel in a shared Figma + repo. Weekly demos, daily Loom updates, and a live staging URL from week one.

  3. 03

    Move

    Launch day is boring on purpose. Then we instrument, iterate, and run paid & SEO loops to compound the work into revenue.

  4. 04

    Maintain

    Optional retainers for product care, content engines, and a monthly experiment cycle. We write the playbook so you can run it without us.

05 — About

A small studio with a long memory.

Brand Mint is a tight team of designers, engineers, and operators who've shipped at agencies, in-house, and at venture-backed startups. We took the parts that worked — senior people, fast cycles, real ownership — and left the rest behind.

We work with founders who care about craft, and operators who want an outside team that moves like an in-house one. We're remote, we're picky about projects, and we keep a calendar you can actually see.

Senior only Every project led by someone with 8+ years.

Studio principles
  1. Ship Fridays. Something visible every week. No black boxes.
  2. Write it down. Decisions live in docs, not in heads or DMs.
  3. Measure or it didn't happen. Every project leaves with a dashboard.
  4. Boring tech, sharp craft. Tools that will still be here in five years.
  5. Hand it back better. You should be more capable when we leave.
06 — FAQ

Common questions.

What does a typical engagement cost?

Marketing sites start at $18k. Custom internal tools start at $24k. Brand systems start at $12k. Retainers start at $6k/mo. Every quote is fixed-scope after the Mint workshop.

How fast can you start?

We hold one or two slots open each month. From signed SOW to kick off is usually under two weeks. Rush slots happen — ask.

Do you work with in-house teams?

Often. We slot in next to in-house design, marketing, or eng teams as a force multiplier — not a replacement. We use your tools and your conventions.

What stack do you use?

For sites: Next.js, Astro, or Shopify Hydrogen, on Vercel. For tools: React + TypeScript, Supabase or Postgres, with Claude or OpenAI where it earns its keep. We pick boring tech on purpose.

Who owns the work?

You do — code, design files, copy, accounts, and dashboards. We hand over a clean repo, a Notion playbook, and a Loom walk through.
