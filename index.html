03 — Process

Crisp, fast, no theater.

Most engagements run 4–8 weeks. We over-communicate in writing, ship every Friday, and keep a single shared dashboard so you always know where things stand.

  1. 01

    Mint

    Half-day strategy intensive at our HITEC City studio (or on video). We map the audience, the offer, and the smallest thing that would move the needle. You leave with a one-page brief and a fixed quote.

  2. 02

    Make

    Design and engineering run in parallel in a shared Figma + repo. Weekly demos, daily Loom updates, and a live staging URL from week one.

  3. 03

    Move

    Launch day is boring on purpose. Then we instrument, iterate, and run paid & SEO loops to compound the work into revenue.

  4. 04

    Maintain

    Optional retainers for product care, content engines, and a monthly experiment cycle. We write the playbook so you can run it without us.

07 — FAQ

Common questions.

What does a typical engagement cost?

We're a startup studio, so the floors are friendly: marketing sites starting from ₹2 lakh ₹1 lakh, custom internal tools starting from ₹4 lakh ₹2 lakh, brand systems starting from ₹1.5 lakh ₹75 K, retainers starting from ₹75 K / month ₹37.5 K / month — all 50% off MRP for the launch cohort. Final quote depends on scope; we match competitive quotations on like-for-like deliverables. Every quote is fixed-scope after the Mint workshop. GST extra.

How fast can you start?

We hold one or two slots open each month. From signed SOW to kick-off is usually under two weeks. Rush slots happen — ask.

Do you work with in-house teams?

Often. We slot in next to in-house design, marketing, or eng teams as a force multiplier — not a replacement. We use your tools and your conventions.

What stack do you use?

For sites: Next.js, Astro, or Shopify Hydrogen, on Vercel. For tools: React + TypeScript, Supabase or Postgres, with Claude or OpenAI where it earns its keep. Razorpay or Stripe for payments. We pick boring tech on purpose.

Can we meet the team in person?

Yes — our studio is in HITEC City, Hyderabad. We host kickoff workshops in person and run weekly demos on video. Travel to Bengaluru, Mumbai, and the Gulf is on us for engagements above ₹5 lakh.

Who owns the work?

You do — code, design files, copy, accounts, and dashboards. We hand over a clean repo, a Notion playbook, and a Loom walkthrough.
