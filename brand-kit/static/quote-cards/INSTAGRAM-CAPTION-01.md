## Why this hook works on IG specifically

Instagram truncates feed-view captions after ~125 characters. The first 116 here are:

> `The fastest founders we know cut one thing: the time between idea and launch. Everything else compounds from there.`

That's the **whole thesis** — fits under the truncation cap, so a founder gets the punchline before the "more" cut-off. The card image and the first line of the caption say the same thing two different ways — image-as-eyebrow, caption-as-body.
