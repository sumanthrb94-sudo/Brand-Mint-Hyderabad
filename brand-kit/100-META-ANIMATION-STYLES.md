## Optimized for Conversions | 3D & 2D Techniques

### CATEGORY 1: KINETIC TYPOGRAPHY & TEXT-DRIVEN (1-15)

1. **Explosive 3D Text Pop** — Isometric 3D text bursts from screen center with motion blur, paired with product reveal
2. **Kinetic Typewriter Scroll** — 2D text types out hook line, auto-scrolls upward as next line appears, CTA locks at bottom
3. **3D Text Rotation Reveal** — Text rotates on Y-axis 360° revealing price/sale info on the back face
4. **Neon Flicker Typography** — 2D neon-style text with randomized opacity flicker (0.1s intervals) for urgency
5. **Text Mask Video Reveal** — Bold text acts as mask, product video plays inside letter shapes
6. **3D Extruded Price Drop** — Price numbers extrude in 3D space, old price shatters, new price slams in
7. **Scrolling Marquee Banner** — 2D infinite horizontal scroll of USPs/benefits across screen bottom
8. **Text Deconstruct Build** — Letters fly in from edges, assemble into headline, then disperse for product shot
9. **3D Perspective Text Tunnel** — Text recedes into Z-depth creating tunnel effect, product emerges from center
10. **Glitch Text Decode** — Text scrambles through random characters before resolving into offer details
11. **Handwritten SVG Trace** — 2D handwritten-style text draws itself on screen with ink stroke animation
12. **3D Floating Letter Cloud** — Individual letters float in 3D space, camera pushes through to form words
13. **Bold Stamp Impact** — 2D text stamps onto screen with screen shake and dust particle burst
14. **Text Split Depth** — Text splits horizontally, top half slides up, bottom slides down, revealing video between
15. **3D Metallic Text Spin** — Chrome/gold 3D text spins on X-axis, reflects product imagery in surface
