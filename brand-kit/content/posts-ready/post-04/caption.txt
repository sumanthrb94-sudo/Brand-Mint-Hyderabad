Modcon Builders is one of those names that hides in plain sight.
Headquartered in Hyderabad with a second base in the UAE. Founded by Chandu Reddy and Manikanta Sridhar Malladi. Known among insiders for Agartha — a project that quietly upgraded what people expect from a Hyderabad address.
In 2024 they took home the Outlook Business Spotlight Entity Award (Business Mint). They didn't run a victory lap. That fits the pattern.
EP04 is a portrait, not a sales pitch. We mapped what Modcon's body of work actually says: a preference for restrained design, a willingness to operate across two geographies without losing the home position, and a brand that lets the architecture speak first.
Three takeaways from the episode:
— design discipline can be a moat
— a UAE footprint is no longer exotic; it's an audience strategy
— the builders who'll matter in 2030 are the ones writing in full sentences in 2026
