Before the towers, there were these five builders.

EP02 of Hyderabad's Empires.

Hyderabad's skyline didn't happen by accident. Before HITEC City was a name, before Gachibowli had a metro stop, before Tellapur had a road in — five builders were already shipping. Some of them still are.

We call them the Heritage Five. Ranks 13 through 09 of our 13-Titans research. They didn't make EP01's top 8, but they outlived almost everyone who did.

In order of reveal:
— Ramky Estates (1994, Ramky Group)
— Sri Aditya Homes (1991, V. Kota Reddy)
— Aditya Constructions (2002, Sathiraju Vakkalanka — different company, different founder than #12, eleven years apart)
— Modi Builders (1985, Modi family)
— SMR Holdings (1993, S. Ram Reddy)

What they share: project discipline, a brand vocabulary that hasn't drifted with the trend cycle, and a reputation among other builders — not just buyers — for finishing what they start.

This is the institutional memory tier. The names on the boards before the boards became billboards.
