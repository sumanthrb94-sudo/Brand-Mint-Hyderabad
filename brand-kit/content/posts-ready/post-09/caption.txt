We're starting a quarterly post: the launch tracker.
The point isn't to predict which project wins. It's to give you a clean snapshot of who launched what, where, and at roughly what positioning — so you can read the city as a portfolio, not a single deal.
Q1 ground rules:
— launches only (not re-launches, not relaunches dressed as launches)
— public listings and RERA filings, cross-checked against builder grids
— each one tagged with locality + segment band (mass / mid / premium / ultra) + a single editorial note from us
Reading the chart, three patterns stood out:
1. Kokapet inventory is still being added even as absorption slows. The price floor is being defended, not the volume.
2. Tellapur saw the highest count of mid-segment launches — quiet movement, big in aggregate.
3. The "premium" band is being repositioned from "amenities" to "design language." The lobby is the new clubhouse.
We'll publish Q2 in early July. If you want the underlying list, comment "tracker" and we'll DM.
