We've been working on a single dashboard for Hyderabad real estate — eight metrics, refreshed quarterly, presented without commentary.
The point is to give you the underlying numbers we use when we form an editorial read, so you can form your own.
The eight metrics:
1. Active project count (citywide)
2. Net new launches this quarter
3. Average per-sqft band — mass / mid / premium / ultra
4. Inventory months — current vs trailing 4Q
5. Top three absorption zones (volume, not value)
6. Top three price-defending zones (value, not volume)
7. Office demand signal — leasing trend that anchors residential
8. Infrastructure tick — one road / one rail / one policy move that changes the next quarter
We won't publish the numbers in this post (each one needs its own sourcing card). We will publish the framework so you know what to look for.
