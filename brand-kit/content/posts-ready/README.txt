BrandMint Studios · 30 Ready-to-Post Instagram Posts
=====================================================

Each post-NN/ folder contains:
  cover.png    1080x1350 hook visual — upload as the post image (or 1st
               carousel slide). For Reels, use as the cover frame.
  caption.txt  Copy-paste-ready caption + hashtags. Open, select-all, paste
               into the IG caption field. Already has the three "."
               breathing dots before the hashtag block.
  meta.txt     Pillar, format, and best post time + day. For your scheduler.

How to post (per day):
  1. Open post-NN/
  2. Upload cover.png to Instagram
  3. Open caption.txt, copy all, paste into the caption field
  4. Refer to meta.txt for the recommended post time

Reels (posts 01, 04, 10, 12, 16, 18, 21, 25, 30): use the cover.png as
the Reel thumbnail/cover; the actual Reel video lives in
brand-kit/video/v13-hyderabad-empires/out/hyderabad-empires-ep0N-*.mp4
(EP01-04 currently available; remaining Reels still to render).
