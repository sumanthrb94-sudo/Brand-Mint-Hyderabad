We took 12 Hyderabad micro-markets and laid them out as a single heatmap.
Not to declare winners. To give you one image you can carry into a conversation.
The bands we use:
— Mass (entry per-sqft band)
— Mid (the wide middle, where most launches happen)
— Premium (where most marketing claims to operate)
— Ultra (where price stops being a primary axis)
The 12 zones in the chart, in no specific order:
Kokapet, Financial District, Gachibowli, Jubilee Hills, Banjara Hills, Hi-Tech City, Madhapur, Manikonda, Narsingi, Tellapur, Bachupally, Kompally.
Each one gets a band assignment and a one-line note on what tilts that band one way or the other.
Caveats up front:
— bands are public-knowledge ranges, not a quote
— micro-pockets vary inside every zone
— the chart is a vocabulary tool, not a transaction tool
