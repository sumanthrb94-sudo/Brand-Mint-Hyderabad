### Post 18

- **#:** 18
- **Pillar:** Brand positioning for developers
- **Format:** Reel (18s)
- **Hook:** Your hoarding is not your brand.
- **Caption:**
A hoarding at the airport exit is not a brand.
A founder feature in a city magazine is not a brand.
A drone shot of the project site is not a brand.
Each one is a tactic. A brand is the consistency across all three — and the next thirty things you do after them.
The fastest way to tell if a builder has a brand or just a media plan:
Hide the logo. Look at the asset.
Could you still identify the builder?
If yes — that's a brand.
If no — that's spend.
Most of Hyderabad is currently spending. Very few are branding.
Comment with a Hyderabad builder you can identify without their logo. We're keeping a list.
Follow @brandmint.studios — provocation reels every other Thursday.
- **Visual concept:** Reel built as a tight 18s sequence. Open on three quick beats of stylised "hoarding / magazine cover / drone-shot frame" — each one rendered as a v13 outline (no real assets), each held for 1.5s with the disqualifying line in serif over it. Then a centre card holding "Hide the logo." (1.5s) → "Look at the asset." (1.5s) → "Can you still tell?" (1.5s). Close on the studio wordmark and a single mint period.
