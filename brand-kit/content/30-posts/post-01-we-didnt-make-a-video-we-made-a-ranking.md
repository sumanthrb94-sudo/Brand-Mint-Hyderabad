EP01 wasn't a reel. It was a position.
Eight builders. One countdown. Three weeks of public-record cross-checks before a single frame was rendered.
The series — Hyderabad's Empires — is our attempt to do for builders what business journalism used to do for companies: rank them, explain them, archive them.
Why a ranking? Because rankings force a point of view. Soft praise floats away. A position lands.
What you saw in EP01:
— eight builders ordered 8 → 1
— no founder voiceover, no PR copy
— just public-record signal (project count, geography, segment focus) and our editorial read on what each one stands for
EP02 went deeper into the Heritage Five. EP03 (out now) is the teaser for the New Money five we'll deep-dive next. EP04 is already up: a portrait of Modcon Builders.
We're not running a city guide. We're building the long-form record of who's shaping Hyderabad — frame by frame.
