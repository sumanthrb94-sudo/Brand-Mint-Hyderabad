### Post 08

- **#:** 08
- **Pillar:** Brand positioning for developers
- **Format:** Carousel (8 slides)
- **Hook:** Lodha doesn't sell flats. It sells a worldview.
- **Caption:**
Look at Lodha's last 30 LinkedIn posts and notice what's missing: floor plans, prices, RERA numbers.
What's there instead: a worldview. "Building a better life." Photography that treats a residential tower like a national monument. Founder commentary that reads more like statecraft than real estate.
That's not a coincidence. It's a positioning choice.
Here's what most Hyderabad builders haven't internalised yet:
At the top of the funnel, no one is making a buying decision. They're forming an opinion of you. The job of brand at that stage is not to inform — it's to make you the answer to a question the buyer hasn't asked yet.
The carousel breaks down what Lodha does in eight moves, and how the same moves translate to a Hyderabad builder at ₹2,000 / sqft, not ₹20,000 / sqft. (You don't need Lodha's budget. You need Lodha's discipline.)
Eight slides. Each one a move you can copy without copying.
