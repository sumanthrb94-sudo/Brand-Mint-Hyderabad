# @brandmint.studios — 30-Day Instagram Content Plan

A production-ready Instagram calendar for **@brandmint.studios** — Hyderabad's editorial real-estate content studio. Built to fuel follower growth, deepen authority on Hyderabad's builder landscape, and quietly position the studio as the obvious agency-of-choice for developers who care how they look.

**Voice rule.** Editorial. Restrained. National Geographic meets Cereal Magazine. No hustle bro. No threadboi. We don't sell, we observe — and the observation does the selling.
**Hook rule.** First 1–3 seconds. Stop the thumb, then earn the next 12.
**Fact rule.** Public record only. If a claim would need an unverified number, we reframe it.
**Visual rule.** Everything is designable in the v13 frame system: warm-black ground (#0E0E0E), paper cream display text (#EAE3D2), mint accent (#9BC9B0), dusty rose echo from Modcon work (#BF7F7F), slim letterbox bars, big serif numerals (Playfair / Tiempos), restrained mono captions.

---

## Posting Cadence Snapshot

- **Base rhythm:** 1 post per day, 30 days, with two double-post days (a Reel + Story-poll) on weeks 2 and 4 to lift engagement signals before a big launch.
- **Anchor slots:** Reels Tue/Thu/Sun, Carousels Mon/Wed/Sat, Single static or Story Fri.
- **Hot-take Stories** (poll/provocation) are deployed mid-week when the feed has gone quiet.
- **No two same-pillar posts back-to-back.** Verified across the master table.
