- **Hook:** Hot take: gated community ≠ premium.
- **Caption:**
A gate is an entry-control system. A premium property is something else entirely.
Somewhere in the last decade, "gated community" became shorthand for "good project." It's not.
There are gated communities in Hyderabad with concrete that's already cracking, amenities that close at 8pm because nobody staffs them past dusk, and a clubhouse the resident WhatsApp group has been complaining about for three years.
There are also un-gated heritage bungalow streets in Banjara Hills where you'd be lucky to get a resale at any price.
"Premium" is the sum of:
— construction discipline (not amenities count)
— design intent (not brochure copy)
— maintenance reality (not handover-day photos)
— and the patience to deliver these without a sale-day discount that quietly tells you the price was inflated to begin with
Use the word more carefully. It's doing more brand work than it deserves.
Disagree? Tell us in the comments. Save if you've ever said "gated community" without thinking.
