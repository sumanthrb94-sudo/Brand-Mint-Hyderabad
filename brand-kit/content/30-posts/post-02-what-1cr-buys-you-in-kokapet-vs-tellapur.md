- **Hook:** What ₹1Cr buys you in Kokapet vs Tellapur.
- **Caption:**
₹1 crore is two different cities depending on the pin code.
In Kokapet, ₹1Cr is now an entry ticket — a smaller 2BHK in a launch tower, often without a balcony view that survives the next year of construction across the road.
In Tellapur, the same ₹1Cr still buys square footage, sometimes a corner unit, with breathing room around the project.
Neither is better. They're different bets on the same decade.
Kokapet says: "I want to be inside the financial spine of the city when it finishes."
Tellapur says: "I want to be on the road that gets there next."
Investors play both sides. End-users almost always pick one and regret not knowing about the other.
We've put six more zones in the carousel — Gachibowli, Narsingi, Tukkuguda, Adibatla, Bachupally, Kompally — with the same simple framing: what you actually get for ₹1Cr in mid-2026, and what the trade-off is.
This isn't price advice. It's a vocabulary post. If you can't articulate the trade-off, you can't negotiate it.
