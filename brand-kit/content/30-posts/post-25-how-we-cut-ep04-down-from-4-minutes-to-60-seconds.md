- **Hook:** How we cut EP04 down from 4 minutes to 60 seconds.
- **Caption:**
The first edit of EP04 ran four minutes. The shipped version is sixty seconds.
Three minutes died in revision. Here's where they went.
Cut 1: the founder backstory section. Interesting, but not a 6-second hook. Moved to a future post.
Cut 2: the second project beat. The portrait gets sharper when one project carries the weight. Kept Agartha. Moved the rest to a series of standalone posts (one of which you saw on Friday).
Cut 3: the awards section. We had the Outlook Business Spotlight Entity Award in the long cut. In the short cut it became one line of supporting copy, not a beat of its own.
Cut 4: the closing. The 4-minute version closed with a montage. The 60-second version closes with one line of type. The montage was emotion. The line was position. Position wins on a 6-second viewing average.
This is the editing principle: every cut you make protects the position.
