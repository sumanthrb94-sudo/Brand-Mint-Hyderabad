## Audience

Hyderabad real-estate **builders and developers** are the buyer.
End buyers and investors are who we **speak to** — so builders see our work
in front of their actual customers. Same logic as B2B media: the readers
aren't the customer, the advertisers are.

- B2B target (the customer): builder marketing heads, founder-led developer
  shops, real-estate agencies in Hyderabad + Gulf.
- B2C echo (the readers): Hyderabad property buyers, investors,
  design-conscious end-users 28-55, English-speaking, India + NRI fringe.

## Funnel

```
Buyer-side content  →  builder sees we reach their buyers  →
                       builder DMs "audit"  →
                       discovery call  →
                       agency engagement (₹2L+ ad-management retainers
                       or ₹6L+ brand+site rebuild)
```

## Tone

Editorial. Restrained. **National Geographic meets Cereal Magazine.**

- No hustle bro.
- No threadboi structures.
- No emoji walls (one earned arrow per caption max).
- Italics for emphasis, not bold.
- Indian context (HITEC City, Q3 2026, INR), never US-centric.
- 9th-grade reading level — we sound smart by being clear, not complex.

## Cadence

1 post/day for 30 days, then assess. Anchor slots:

- **Reels** — Tue / Thu / Sun (highest organic reach windows)
- **Carousels** — Mon / Wed / Sat (save-optimised)
- **Story-polls / hot takes** — Fri (and mid-week filler)

No two same-pillar posts back-to-back.

## Monetization

Two streams:

1. **Agency services** (primary) — full-service social-media-ads management
   + brand design for real-estate clients. Posts route to DM "audit" CTA.
2. **Brand deals / sponsorship** (secondary) — builders pay us to produce
   a sponsored Empires-format episode about them. EP04 (Modcon) is the
   template; future paid episodes follow that format.

Pricing never appears in feed posts. Route to DM.

## Hard avoids

- **No founder face content.** No selfie Reels, no talking-head clips. Stays editorial / faceless.
- **No invented numbers.** Public-record only. If a claim needs an unverified stat, reframe it.
- **No competitor callouts** (named or implied).
- **No pricing in public posts** — route to DM.
- **No trending audio / dance trends.**
- **No generic real-estate advice** (10-tips-for-first-time-buyers, etc.).
- **No mindset / motivational content.**
- **No stock photos.**

## Cold-start (under 1K) addenda

Because the account is in cold-start, every post optimises for *signals*
the algorithm uses to classify and promote a new account:

- **Saves first.** Captions end with a save prompt. Carousels last slide
  is always "Save · Share · DM audit".
- **Niche-tight only.** Don't post broad-lifestyle filler — the algorithm
  is still learning what we are. Stay strictly in builder + locality lanes
  for the first 60 days.
- **Hashtag tiers.** Mix of small (10K-100K) + mid (100K-500K) tags.
  Avoid mega-tier (#realestate, #property, #design) — drowned out.
  Always include Hyderabad-specific tags (#hyderabadrealestate,
  #kokapet, #financialdistrict, etc.).
- **DM keywords.** Every brand-positioning + BTS post mentions a DM
  keyword ("audit", "brand", "EP") so DM volume can be tracked.
- **No story-only posting weeks.** Stories don't help cold-start reach.
  Use them as engagement spice, not primary content.

## When in doubt

Lean **editorial over promotional.** The pitch is the work, not the words.
A reader who saves the post because the framing was sharp is worth more
than a reader who clicks the link because the CTA was loud.
