## Cold-start hybrid — the risk

Multi-niche accounts under 1K are hard to classify. The IG algorithm
relies on engagement-pattern signals to decide who to show the account to;
mixing two distinct niches confuses that signal and slows growth in both.

**Mitigation — phased rollout:**

1. **Days 1-30:** Lead with Track A (real-estate). The 30-post plan, EP01-04,
   and the carousels already shipped fill this window. Account classifies
   as Hyderabad real-estate editorial.
2. **Days 31-60:** Introduce Track B (software/dev) at a 1-in-5 frequency.
   Each Track B post uses an unmistakable visual differentiator (terminal
   green-on-black, monospaced display type) so viewers code it as a
   separate lane.
3. **Day 60+:** If signals are healthy on both tracks, balance to
   ~60% Track A / 40% Track B. If Track A is dominating, keep it at 75/25
   and revisit at 5K followers.

## Tone

Editorial. Restrained. **National Geographic meets Cereal Magazine.**
Same voice across both tracks — only the subject and visual mode shifts.

- No hustle bro.
- No threadboi structures.
- No emoji walls (one earned arrow per caption max).
- Italics for emphasis, not bold.
- Indian context (HITEC City, Q3 2026, INR), never US-centric.
- 9th-grade reading level — we sound smart by being clear, not complex.

## Cadence

1 post/day. Anchor slots:

- **Reels** — Tue / Thu / Sun (highest organic reach windows)
- **Carousels** — Mon / Wed / Sat (save-optimised)
- **Story-polls / hot takes** — Fri (and mid-week filler)

No two same-track-and-same-pillar posts back-to-back.

## Cold-start (under 1K) addenda

- **Saves first.** Captions end with a save prompt. Carousel last slide is
  always `Save · Share · DM <keyword>`.
- **Niche-tight only in days 1-30.** Don't introduce Track B yet — let
  Track A classify the account first.
- **Hashtag tiers.** Mix of small (10K-100K) + mid (100K-500K). Avoid
  mega-tier. Always include Hyderabad-specific tags on Track A.
- **DM keywords.** Track A uses `audit`. Track B will use `BUILT`. Both
  are tracked weekly.
- **No story-only posting weeks.** Stories don't help cold-start reach.

## When in doubt

Lean **editorial over promotional.** The pitch is the work, not the words.
