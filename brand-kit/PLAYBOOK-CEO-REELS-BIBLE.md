## 1. VIRAL TRANSITIONS CATALOGUE

### Core Transitions (Use These Daily)

| Transition | What It Is | When to Use | Production Note |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Jump Cut** | Quick cut between scenes, same subject | Talking-head content, listicles, reactions | Film 10 raw clips, batch-edit in CapCut. One cut every 1.5–3 seconds max. |
| **Zoom Transition** | Zoom out from Scene A → zoom in to Scene B | Location changes, outfit swaps, before/after | Use keyframe zoom in CapCut/Premiere. Speed ramp the zoom for impact. |
| **Text Wipe** | Text covers screen → clears to reveal next scene | Educational content, "3 Tips" formats, tutorials | Bold sans-serif font, 80% screen coverage, 0.3s wipe duration. |
| **Align/Ghost Match** | Ghost overlay of last frame to line up next shot | Outfit changes, object swaps, continuity magic | Instagram's built-in Align tool or manual opacity overlay in editing. |
| **Beat Drop Cut** | Hard cut synced exactly to audio beat drop | Music-driven content, hype reveals, product launches | Mark beats on timeline. Cut 2 frames before the drop for tension. |
| **Spin/Orbit** | Camera or subject rotates 360° into next scene | Fashion, travel, lifestyle reveals | Film on gimbal or use post-production rotation + motion blur. |
| **Mask Reveal** | Object or hand wipes across screen to unveil | Product reveals, "wait for it" content | Use green screen or manual rotoscoping. Hand-to-camera is most viral. |
| **Speed Ramp** | Slow motion → normal speed (or reverse) | Action moments, sports, unboxing drama | Shoot 60fps minimum. Ramp from 10% to 100% over 8 frames. |
| **Seamless Loop** | End frame matches start frame for infinite replay | Satisfying content, ASMR, process videos | Plan first and last shot identically. Algorithm rewards replays. |
| **Match Cut** | Similar shape/color/action bridges two scenes | Cinematic storytelling, brand montages | Match eye lines, product shapes, or hand gestures across cuts. |

### Advanced Transitions (For Premium Content)

| Transition | What It Is | CEO Use Case |
|---|---|---|
| **Dolly Zoom (Vertigo)** | Camera moves in while zooming out | Property tours, "scale shift" moments for real estate |
| **Morph Cut** | Seamless blend between two talking-head clips | Long-form educational content without visible cuts |
| **Strobe Flash** | White flash on beat, scene changes | High-energy product drops, event promos |
| **Glitch Data-Burst** | Digital distortion transition | Tech content, AI/crypto ventures |
| **Paper Tear / Film Burn** | Analog texture overlay transition | Luxury brands, vintage aesthetic |
| **3D Camera Push** | Push through screen into next scene | App demos, website walkthroughs |

## 2. PRODUCTION SPECS & TECHNICAL STANDARDS

### Non-Negotiable Specs (Feed This to Claude)

```
FORMAT: 9:16 vertical (1080×1920)
LENGTH: 7–15 seconds for virality; 30–60 for education
FRAME RATE: 30fps standard; 60fps for speed ramps/slow-mo
BITRATE: 8–15 Mbps for crisp export
CAPTIONS: Burned-in, large bold text (minimum 72pt mobile-safe)
SAFE ZONES: Keep text within central 1080×1420 area (avoid top 250px and bottom 130px for UI overlap)
AUDIO: -14 LUFS integrated loudness, true peak -1 dB
THUMBNAIL: 1080×1350 (4:5 crop) for profile grid consistency
```

### The 3-Second Hook Rule

Your first frame must contain one of these:
- **Pattern Interrupt**: Unexpected visual (flash, color shift, extreme close-up)
- **Text Hook**: "The mistake that cost me ₹50L" / "Why I fired my entire team"
- **Audio Hook**: Start mid-song at the chorus/beat drop, not the intro
- **Result-First**: Show the end state, then rewind to "how I got here"

## 3. CREATOR TERMINOLOGY GLOSSARY

Drop these terms into your Claude prompts for precision:

| Term | Meaning | Prompt Usage |
|---|---|---|
| **B-Roll** | Supplementary footage cut over primary content | "Add B-roll of Dubai skyline during voiceover" |
| **J-Cut / L-Cut** | Audio leads video (J) or video leads audio (L) | "Use J-cuts for smoother talking-head transitions" |
| **Keyframing** | Setting animation points for motion/scale/opacity | "Keyframe text scale from 0% to 120% with overshoot" |
| **Speed Ramping** | Variable playback speed within single clip | "Speed ramp the unboxing from 0.5x to 2x on reveal" |
| **Lower Third** | Text graphic in bottom third of frame | "Add branded lower third with name and title" |
| **Motion Tracking** | Lock graphic to moving object | "Motion track the price tag to the product" |
| **Chroma Key** | Green screen removal | "Chroma key the background, replace with luxury office" |
| **Parallax** | Background moves slower than foreground | "Add parallax depth to the 2D image" |
| **Kinetic Typography** | Animated text as primary visual | "Use kinetic typography for the quote reveal" |
| **Stinger** | Short branded animation at video start/end | "Add 2-second stinger with logo reveal" |
| **LUT** | Color grading preset | "Apply teal-orange LUT for premium feel" |
| **Safe Margins** | Visible boundary guides for text placement | "Keep all text within 80% safe margins" |

## 5. COPY-PASTE CLAUDE PROMPTS

### For Video Scripts:

```
"Generate a 12-second Instagram Reel script for [BRAND/PRODUCT].
Hook: Pattern interrupt with extreme close-up.
Structure: Jump cut every 1.5 seconds.
Transition: Beat drop cut at 0:08.
CTA: Text wipe revealing 'Link in bio' at 0:10.
Tone: Aggressive CEO energy.
Include: Kinetic typography for key stats, lower third for credibility."
```

### For Graphic Design:

```
"Create a 1080×1920 Reel cover graphic.
Style: Dark luxury aesthetic, gold accents, parallax depth.
Text: [HEADLINE] in bold sans-serif, 80pt, centered in safe margins.
Elements: Subtle motion blur on edges, vignette overlay.
Brand: Minimalist, expensive-looking, institutional."
```

### For Editing Instructions:

```
"Edit this raw footage into a viral Reel:
1. Trim first 3 seconds to strongest visual hook
2. Apply jump cuts every 2 seconds maximum
3. Add beat-synced zoom transition at midpoint
4. Burn in captions with bold yellow text, black stroke
5. End with seamless loop back to frame 1
6. Export 9:16, 30fps, H.264, under 15 seconds"
```

## 6. VIRAL CONTENT FORMULAS

| Format | Structure | Length | Best For |
|---|---|---|---|
| **The Contrarian** | "Everyone says X. Here's why they're wrong." | 15–30s | Thought leadership, personal brand |
| **The Process** | Start with result → flashback to chaos | 30–60s | Behind-the-scenes, agency work |
| **The Listicle** | "3 things I wish I knew before..." | 12–20s | Education, quick value |
| **The Reaction** | Genuine reaction to comment/result | 7–15s | Relatability, algorithm boost |
| **The Transformation** | Before → After with hard cut | 10–15s | Product demos, fitness, real estate |
| **The POV** | "POV: You're my [role] on day 1" | 10–20s | Recruitment, culture, aspirational |

## 7. CEO EXECUTION CHECKLIST

1. **Batch film 10 clips in 2 hours** — don't edit one-by-one
2. **Apply one signature transition across all** — brand consistency
3. **Never exceed one transition type per Reel** — over-editing kills virality
4. **Captions are non-negotiable** — 80% watch without sound
5. **Test 7–15s vs 30–60s** — algorithm rewards completion rate, not length
6. **Use trending audio for 30% of content** — original audio for 60%, experimental 10%
7. **Post cover in 4:5 (1080×1350)** — profile grid must look intentional, not accidental
