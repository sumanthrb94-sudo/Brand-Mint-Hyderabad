## A/B variants (cycle every 14 days)

Hold the body identical. Swap only the opening hook.

| Cell | Opening hook | Mechanism |
|------|--------------|-----------|
| A *(control)* | `5 signs your template is the bottleneck. If two feel familiar, your storefront is leaving money on the table.` | Listicle + self-audit |
| B *(antagonist)* | `Your Shopify theme isn't the problem. The fact that you can't tell where the brand ends and the template begins — that's the problem.` | Anti-positioning · for theme-fatigued founders |
| C *(price-led)* | `Templates cost ₹999. The orders you lost because of one cost ₹9 lakh. Five signs you're paying the second invoice.` | Loss-framed · for high-revenue founders |
| D *(geography)* | `Built in HITEC City for founders who outgrew the template. Five signs you're one of them.` | Geo-led + audience-flag |

**Winner metric:** Saves / 1,000 impressions, *then* DMs / 1k. Listicles live or die on saves; the DM rate tells you the conversion.

If hook rate < 22% after 1,000 impressions → swap to **B** (antagonist).
If DMs / 1k < 0.4 → swap to **C** (price-led, qualifies harder).
