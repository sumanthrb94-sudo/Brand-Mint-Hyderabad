## Why this hook works on Instagram specifically

Instagram truncates feed-view captions after ~125 characters. The first 124 of this caption are **three specific INR price claims**:

> `₹1 lakh for a custom website. ₹2 lakh for a custom CRM. ₹75K for a brand system. 50% off MRP for the launch cohort.`

That fits *just* under the truncation point, so in the grid-scroll view a founder sees the concrete numbers before the "more" cut-off — not a generic positioning line. Specificity-led hooks out-convert benefit-led hooks in the founder/B2B niche by ~30-40% on cold IG audiences.

## A/B variants to rotate (cycle every 14 days)

Hold the rest of the caption identical. Swap only the opening hook.

| Cell | Opening hook | Mechanism |
|------|--------------|-----------|
| A *(control)* | `₹1 lakh for a custom website. ₹2 lakh for a custom CRM. ₹75K for a brand system. 50% off MRP for the launch cohort.` | Specificity-led |
| B *(antagonist)* | `Stop renting your business logic from SaaS companies. We build the platform. You own it. 50% off launch.` | Anti-positioning |
| C *(math)* | `SaaS costs ₹18 L over 3 years. Custom costs ₹6.5 L once. We're now half of that for the launch cohort.` | Math-led — for mid-renewal audience |
| D *(scarcity)* | `Six services. 50% off. First 10 founders this quarter. No exceptions, no extensions. Booking → DM "BUILT".` | Scarcity-led |

**Winner metric:** DMs / 1,000 impressions (not raw visits). Volume rewards loud hooks; DM-conversion rewards the right ones.
