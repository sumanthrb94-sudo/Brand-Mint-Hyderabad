02 · Placement & rules

Clear space, sizing, don’ts

Clear space

Reserve clear space equal to the height of the M-stroke on every side. Nothing — text, image edges, illustration — encroaches.

Minimum sizes

Primary lock-up120px / 32mm
Wordmark only96px / 24mm
Monogram16px / 5mm
On-screen 4K bug≥ 64px

When to use which

Light bg → primary. Dark bg → primary-dark. Logo strips → wordmark. Square/avatar → monogram. 1-colour → monogram-mono.

✓ Do

  • Keep the gradient direction top-left → bottom-right.
  • Use a translucent backdrop over photography/footage.
  • Pair mint CTAs with ink text on paper.
  • Let gold stay quieter than mint, always.

✕ Don’t

  • Recolour the gradient — use a mono variant instead.
  • Stretch, skew, rotate, or outline the mark.
  • Add drop shadows or bevels.
  • Place the mark on busy footage with no backdrop.
  • Change the wordmark font (Plus Jakarta Sans 600).

Safe areas: all text within 90% title-safe; lower-third above bottom 12%. Bug ≥ 64px @4K, 40–60% opacity. Keep AI-burned text out — composite logos in post so they stay crisp.

Founder-to-founder, calm, specific. Indian-English warmth. Lead with a number, italicise one word, sign off “— Brand Mint, Hyderabad”.

Master video prompt

Netflix-style documentary cinematography, photoreal, ultra-HD. A confident founder-presenter [USE ATTACHED AVATAR — exact likeness, face-locked] in a minimal premium studio in HITEC City, Hyderabad. SKIN: hyper-real texture, visible pores, subsurface scattering, no plastic smoothing, tack-sharp eyes, 4K crispness. ENVIRONMENT: deep emerald-ink walls (#0B1F1A), cream accents (#F5F1EA), bokeh mint dashboards behind, volumetric haze, mint gradient glow. CAMERA: 35mm prime, T1.8, slow push-in dolly over 10s, gimbal-smooth, rack focus to eyes by 2s. ARRI Alexa look, 4K, 24fps. LIGHTING: 3-point — 5600K soft silk key L, 3200K warm fill R, crisp emerald-mint (#10B981) rim, one mint practical glow. Low-key premium Netflix-doc mood, catchlights in both eyes. GRADE: teal-emerald shadows, warm cream highlights, rich blacks, fine 35mm grain. Palette mint #10B981/#7CF6C8, ink #0A0E0C, paper #F5F1EA, one gold #C9A14A accent. BEATS (10s): 0–2 profile→turn + focus; 2–6 push-in + line to camera; 6–8.5 confident micro-nod; 8.5–10 held hero frame, negative space upper-left for logo. NEGATIVE: plastic/waxy skin, over-smoothed, beauty filter, warped hands, text artifacts, watermark, logo distortion, oversaturated, low-res, cartoon, 3D-render look.
