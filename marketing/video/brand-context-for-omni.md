## 6 · Voice & tone (apply to all copy/VO Omni generates)

- **Three-word identity:** Senior. Specific. Indian.
- Confident without arrogance · specific over abstract · warm but not buddy-buddy · editorial, never salesy · founder-to-founder always.
- **Lead with a number.** Italicise exactly **one** word per headline. Sign off **"— Brand Mint, Hyderabad."**
- **NEVER use these words:** innovative, solutions, cutting-edge, disruptive, synergy, holistic, leverage, paradigm, game-changer, world-class, best-in-class, next-gen, scalable, seamless, turnkey, end-to-end, craftsmanship, forward-thinking, ecosystem, transform, empower, unlock, reimagine. (They signal "agency that bills hourly," not "studio that ships.")

## 7 · Visual identity (lock the look)

- **Colours:** Mint 3 `#10B981` (primary), Mint 2 `#7CF6C8`, Ink `#0A0E0C`, Paper/cream `#F5F1EA`, dark interlude BM Ink `#0B1F1A` + Emerald `#00C897`, Gold `#C9A14A` (one editorial accent only).
- **Type:** Plus Jakarta Sans (display/titles), Inter (body), JetBrains Mono (all numerals, ₹, %, dates).
- **Logo:** mint-gradient circle holding a single-stroke "M" that reads as a rising graph line; lock-up = mark + "Brand Mint" + "— Hyderabad". Composite the real logo in post; don't let AI render it. Clear space = height of the M-stroke.
- **Imagery:** dark editorial + warm cream; product mockups of shipped sites/tools, NOT stock "people pointing at laptops." India-grounded if using photography (HITEC City). No stock-y smiles.
