# Brand Mint — Voiceover Scripts (Sarvam AI / Bulbul TTS)

Paste-ready VO scripts. Number words are spelled out and punctuation is tuned so
Sarvam paces and pronounces cleanly. On-screen text can keep symbols (₹, %, etc.) —
these are the spoken versions.

## Recommended Sarvam settings
- Model: `bulbul:v2` · Language: `en-IN` (use `hi-IN` for the Hinglish cut)
- Voice: audition a calm one — warm male (Abhilash / Karun) or female (Anushka / Vidya)
- Pace: `0.9` · Pitch: `0` · Loudness: `1.0`
- Enable preprocessing / number normalization: ON
- `...` = dramatic pause · commas = micro-breaths
- Written deliberately: `brandmint dot studio`, `Hi-Tech City`, amounts as words

We don't hand you files... we hand you brands that compound. A senior studio in Hyderabad — custom websites and bespoke tools, built to ship. Brand Mint.

Most studios hand you files. We hand you outcomes. Brand Mint is a senior-led studio in Hyderabad. We design custom websites, and build the internal tools that quietly run behind them. Priced in rupees... built to ship. Start a project, at brandmint dot studio.

Last year, we influenced over five hundred crore rupees in client revenue. If you'd rather pay for taste than for overhead... let's talk. Brand Mint. Hyderabad.

Hum custom websites design karte hain, aur woh internal tools banate hain jo aapki team chalati hai. Rupees mein priced... weeks mein delivered. Project shuru kijiye — brandmint dot studio par.

### Brand voice reminders
Lead with a number, keep one emphasis per line, sign off "Brand Mint. Hyderabad."
Never use: innovative, solutions, world-class, seamless, end-to-end, etc.
Swap any placeholder metric (e.g. "five hundred crore") for a real, current figure
before publishing a paid ad.
