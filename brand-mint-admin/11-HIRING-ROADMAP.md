# 11 · Hiring Roadmap

The first 6 hires, in order, with target start months, comp ranges, and minimum bars.

## Hiring philosophy

1. **Hire senior, slow.** Every hire must be able to ship a project end-to-end without supervision within 60 days. If they can't, they're not senior.
2. **One hire at a time.** Two simultaneous hires double the onboarding cost without doubling output. Hire #2 only after #1 is fully self-sufficient.
3. **Try before you marry.** Every hire starts with a paid 4-week project (₹40-80K depending on scope). Convert based on the work, not the resume.
4. **Pay above market for senior, below for junior.** We don't hire juniors yet, so this is theoretical until Year 3.
5. **Equity over cash for first 5 hires.** ESOP pool of 10% set aside Day 1.

## Job descriptions

### Hire #1 — Senior Designer

**What they own:** Visual direction across every engagement. Brand systems, site design, type/colour craft, design ops.

**Must-have:**
- 6+ years across brand identity AND digital product (not just one)
- Portfolio with at least 2 systems shipped end-to-end (logo → site → templates)
- Fluent in Figma + a vector tool (Illustrator / Affinity)
- Has shipped on actual revenue-generating sites; can speak to conversion impact
- Writes well — design without writing is decoration

**Nice-to-have:**
- Motion / Lottie / Rive experience
- Has freelanced solo (used to client-facing accountability)
- Indian + global brand portfolio

**Disqualifiers:**
- Only-agency career (never solo client-facing)
- "Designer who can't talk to clients" — non-starter
- More illustration than systems thinking

**Comp:** ₹15-21 L base (₹1.25-1.75 L/mo) + 1.5% ESOP (4-year vest, 1-year cliff) + ₹2 L equipment budget

**Trial:** 4-week paid engagement on an actual client at ₹50-80K. Ship a brand system + 4 design screens. Convert based on craft + collaboration.

### Hire #2 — Senior Full-stack Engineer

**What they own:** Build for every engagement that has code. Architecture, deploy, performance, AI integrations.

**Must-have:**
- 6+ years across React/Next.js + Node + at least one other backend (Python, Go, Rails)
- Has shipped a production AI feature (LLM integration, RAG, or similar)
- Comfortable with Postgres / Supabase, Vercel / Cloudflare, observability stacks
- Can write a 3-page architecture doc
- Has worked on small teams (≤5) — hates process for process's sake

**Nice-to-have:**
- Open-source contributions
- Speaking / writing about engineering
- Has built a marketing site that hit Lighthouse 95+

**Disqualifiers:**
- FAANG career only with no early-stage shipping
- Can't speak to product trade-offs
- Slow IDE reflexes (we move fast on demos)

**Comp:** ₹22-30 L base + 2.0% ESOP + ₹2 L equipment

**Trial:** 4-week paid engagement at ₹80K-1.2 L. Ship a full feature on a real client tool.

### Hire #3 — Studio Manager / Producer

**What they own:** Project ops. Kickoffs, demos, client comms, invoicing, hiring pipeline. The studio's chief of staff.

**Must-have:**
- 4+ years agency / studio ops or product management
- Notion / Linear / Slack power user
- Has run client comms for at least 5 simultaneous projects
- Comfortable in INR finances + Indian compliance (or willing to learn fast)
- Writes clearly under pressure

**Nice-to-have:**
- Has run founder offices or chief-of-staff roles
- Knows GST / TDS / vendor compliance
- Multilingual (English + Hindi minimum, plus one regional)

**Disqualifiers:**
- "I'd rather not be on client calls"
- Treats ops as paperwork instead of leverage

**Comp:** ₹10-14 L base + 0.5% ESOP

### Hire #4 — Senior Brand Strategist

**What they own:** Discovery, positioning, messaging, voice. The strategic spine of every brand engagement.

**Must-have:**
- 7+ years brand strategy at agencies or in-house
- Has facilitated at least 30 client workshops
- Writes essays / has thought-leadership presence
- Indian + global brand intuition

**Comp:** ₹18-24 L base + 1.0% ESOP

### Hire #5 — Performance Marketing Lead

**What they own:** Performance media retainer delivery. Meta + Google + LinkedIn ad ops. Reporting.

**Must-have:**
- 5+ years managing >₹1 Cr/year of paid spend
- Hands-on (not just strategy) — must build campaigns themselves
- Has scaled D2C brands or B2B SaaS specifically

**Comp:** ₹15-21 L base + 1.0% ESOP

### Hire #6 — AI Engineer

**What they own:** AI integration product line. Eval suites, model selection, observability, fine-tuning if needed.

**Must-have:**

- Knows the cost/quality trade-offs across Claude / GPT / Gemini intimately

**Comp:** ₹24-32 L base + 1.5% ESOP

## Trial-engagement → conversion process

1. **Sourcing call (30 min)** — discuss role + check chemistry
2. **Paid take-home (2-4 hours, ₹5-10K honorarium)** — single, well-defined task. Reviewed by founder.
3. **Working session (90 min)** — pair on something real. The interview-as-collaboration test.
4. **References (3 minimum)** — call the references *yourself*. Always uncover one weakness.
5. **Trial offer** — 4-week paid engagement at 60-80% of target comp. Mutual evaluation.
6. **Conversion** — full offer or part ways. Be ruthless. Either side can walk.

## ESOP terms (default)

- **Pool:** 10% of fully diluted equity
- **Vesting:** 4 years, 1-year cliff
- **Strike:** FMV at grant
- **Acceleration:** 1-year acceleration on company sale (single trigger)
- **Exit window:** 90 days post-departure to exercise (extend to 5 years if employee asks — almost always say yes)

Document in writing on day 1. Use a startup-friendly lawyer to template the agreement; reuse for every grant.

## Firing — how we do it

If a hire isn't working out:
- **Conversation 1 (week 4-6):** specific feedback, named gaps, 30-day plan
- **Conversation 2 (week 10):** are gaps closing? If not, mutually agree on parting
- **Severance:** 1 month for trial-period exits, 2 months after first 90 days

Never let a struggling hire linger past 90 days. Bad fits compound into resentment.
