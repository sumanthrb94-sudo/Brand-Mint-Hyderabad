## Bundle discounts

When a client books **2+ services in a single SOW**, apply 10% bundle discount to the smaller line item.

Example: Site (₹3.5 L) + Brand System (₹2 L) = ₹5.3 L instead of ₹5.5 L.

Don't discount more than 10%. If they push, walk away.

## Free / unbillable work

- Discovery call (30 min)

- 1 logo concept variation in pitch deck

Anything beyond this gets quoted.

## When to refer out

We don't do these — refer to a partner and take a 10% kickback or just goodwill:
- Print collateral / packaging design → refer to a print specialist
- Video production beyond 1 short clip → refer to a video studio
- Performance creative for TV/OOH → not our lane
- Pure copywriting engagements → refer
- App development (native iOS/Android) → refer
