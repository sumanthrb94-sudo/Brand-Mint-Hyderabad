**MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT**

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of `[DATE]` (the "Effective Date") between:

**Brand Mint Studio Private Limited**, a company incorporated under the Companies Act, 2013, with its registered office at `[ADDRESS, HITEC CITY, HYDERABAD, TELANGANA – 500081]` (hereinafter "Studio")

AND

`[CLIENT LEGAL NAME]`, having its registered office at `[CLIENT ADDRESS]` (hereinafter "Client")

(Studio and Client are together the "Parties" and individually a "Party".)

---

## 1. Scope of services

1.1 Studio shall provide creative, design, engineering, and consulting services as mutually agreed in one or more Statements of Work ("SOW") executed under this Agreement.

1.2 Each SOW shall reference this Agreement and detail: (a) deliverables, (b) timeline, (c) fees, (d) acceptance criteria, (e) Studio personnel.

1.3 In the event of conflict between an SOW and this Agreement, the SOW shall prevail for that engagement.

## 2. Fees and payment

2.1 Fees are denominated in **Indian Rupees (INR)** and exclusive of applicable Goods and Services Tax (GST), which shall be added at the prevailing rate.

2.2 Standard payment schedule is **50% advance** upon SOW execution and **50% on delivery**, unless the SOW specifies otherwise.

2.3 Studio shall raise GST-compliant tax invoices. Payment terms: Net-30 from invoice date.

2.4 Overdue invoices accrue interest at **2% per month** from the due date until cleared.

2.5 Out-of-pocket expenses (third-party tools, hosting, travel beyond Hyderabad) shall be pre-approved by Client and reimbursed at cost.

## 3. Term and termination

3.1 This Agreement is effective from the Effective Date and continues until terminated in accordance with this Section 3.

3.2 Either Party may terminate any active SOW with **fifteen (15) days' written notice**. On termination, Client shall pay for all work completed up to the termination date.

3.3 Either Party may terminate this Agreement (and all active SOWs) immediately upon material breach by the other Party that remains uncured 30 days after written notice.

## 4. Intellectual property

4.1 **Final deliverables**: upon receipt of full payment for an SOW, Studio assigns to Client all right, title, and interest in the final deliverables (e.g., final logo, site code, design files) for that SOW, except as set out in 4.2.

4.2 **Studio retains ownership of** (a) Studio's pre-existing tools, methodologies, libraries, prompt templates, internal frameworks, and the Studio's own brand and marketing assets, regardless of whether any of these are used in deliverables; and (b) ideas, concepts, and explorations not selected by Client as final deliverables.

4.3 **Studio's portfolio rights**: Studio retains the right to display final deliverables and an outcome summary in its portfolio, case studies, and marketing materials, subject to any reasonable confidentiality requirements specified by Client in writing.

## 5. Confidentiality

5.1 Each Party shall keep the other's confidential information in strict confidence and use it only for the purpose of performing this Agreement.

5.2 Confidentiality obligations survive termination for **three (3) years**.

5.3 The obligation does not apply to information that (a) is or becomes public through no fault of the receiving party, (b) was known to the receiving party prior to disclosure, or (c) is required to be disclosed by law.

## 6. Warranties and disclaimers

6.1 Studio warrants that services will be performed in a professional manner consistent with industry standards.

6.2 Studio provides a **30-day post-delivery warranty** for bug fixes on engineered deliverables (sites, tools). Beyond 30 days, fixes are billed hourly or via retainer.

6.3 EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY STATED, ALL DELIVERABLES ARE PROVIDED "AS IS." STUDIO DISCLAIMS ALL OTHER WARRANTIES, EXPRESS OR IMPLIED.

## 7. Limitation of liability

7.1 Studio's total aggregate liability arising out of or relating to this Agreement shall not exceed **the fees paid by Client under the affected SOW in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim**.

7.2 Neither Party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages.

## 8. Indemnity

8.1 Client shall indemnify Studio against claims arising from (a) Client's content, materials, or instructions, or (b) Client's use of deliverables in violation of applicable law.

8.2 Studio shall indemnify Client against claims that the deliverables infringe a third party's intellectual property rights, except where such infringement results from Client-supplied materials or third-party assets identified to Client.

## 9. Independent contractor

9.1 Studio is an independent contractor. Nothing in this Agreement creates a partnership, joint venture, agency, or employment relationship.

## 10. Force majeure

10.1 Neither Party is liable for failure to perform due to causes beyond its reasonable control (acts of God, government action, internet/infrastructure failure, pandemics).

## 11. Governing law and dispute resolution

11.1 This Agreement is governed by the laws of India.

11.2 Disputes shall first be attempted to be resolved through good-faith negotiation between the Parties' senior representatives within 30 days.

11.3 Failing resolution, disputes shall be submitted to **arbitration under the Arbitration and Conciliation Act, 1996** at Hyderabad. The arbitrator shall be a sole arbitrator mutually appointed by the Parties.

## 12. Miscellaneous

- **Notices:** sent by email to authorised representatives at the addresses listed in the SOW.
- **Assignment:** neither Party may assign without prior written consent, except to a successor entity.
- **Amendment:** any modification must be in writing and signed.
- **Severability:** if any clause is invalid, the rest remains in force.
- **Entire agreement:** this Agreement, together with active SOWs, is the entire understanding.
