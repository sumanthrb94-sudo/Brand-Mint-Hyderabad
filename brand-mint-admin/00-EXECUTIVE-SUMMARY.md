## What we are

Brand Mint is a senior-led digital studio in HITEC City, Hyderabad. We design and engineer **custom websites, internal tools, brand systems, and the AI integrations that quietly run behind them.** Every engagement is led by an 8+ year operator. We charge in INR with startup-friendly floors (₹2 L for a marketing site; ₹4 L for a custom internal tool; ₹75 K/mo for retainers).

## Tagline

> **We mint brands that compound.**

## Why we win

1. **Senior-only delivery.** No interns at the table. The person who pitches you is the person who builds.
2. **One studio, full stack.** Strategy + brand + site + tools + retainer in one room, so handoffs don't kill velocity.
3. **Founder-friendly pricing.** ₹2–10 L floors that growth-stage founders can actually approve. Premium quality, startup tier.
4. **India-grounded.** INR billing, GST-compliant, IST hours, Hyderabad ground game. Plus working hours that overlap UK and the Gulf.
5. **Ship-first culture.** Six-week MVPs not six-month retainers. We bias toward live URLs.

## Who we serve

Founder-led companies in India and the Gulf, ₹2 Cr–₹500 Cr revenue, who want creative work that ships fast and pays back. Sweet spot: Series A/B Indian SaaS, D2C brands at ₹5 Cr+ ARR, Gulf-based real estate / hospitality / fashion houses.

## Who we don't serve

- Logo-only briefs
- Anything we can't tie to revenue or product velocity
- Sub-₹1 L engagements (we lose money)
- "Make it look like Apple" without a strategy
