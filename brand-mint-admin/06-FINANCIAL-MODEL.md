## Break-even math

Fixed monthly costs (after H2 with full team): ~₹6.5 L/mo.

Break-even revenue per month: **₹6.5 L**.

That's roughly 2 small projects (₹3 L each) OR 1 medium project + 3 retainers OR 4-5 retainers alone.

Once 4 retainers are signed at ₹1.5 L/mo, base load (₹6 L) covers ~92% of fixed costs. Every project after that flows mostly to profit.

---

## Cash flow rules

- Always invoice **50% advance** before work starts — no exceptions
- Net-30 terms on second invoice; charge **2% interest/mo on overdue**
- Maintain **3 months of fixed costs** in business savings as runway buffer (~₹20 L by M9)
- Never borrow against projected revenue
- Founder draw matches business cash; if cash drops below 2-month buffer, founder draw freezes first

## Critical numbers to track weekly

1. **Cash on hand** (₹)
2. **Pipeline value** (sum of all stages 3+, weighted)
3. **MRR from retainers**
4. **Open AR** (unpaid invoices)
5. **Burn rate** (rolling 3-month avg)

If any of these moves >20% week-over-week, the founder gets a notification and a 30-min review is scheduled within 48 hrs.
