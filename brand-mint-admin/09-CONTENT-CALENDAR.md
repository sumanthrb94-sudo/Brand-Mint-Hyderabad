# 09 · Content Calendar — 30 days

The studio's voice, channelled into a repeatable content engine. Ship 4 LinkedIn posts a week, 1 long-form a month, 1 case study a month.

## Channels & cadence

| Channel | Frequency | Best time IST | Owner |
|---|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn (founder) | 4×/week | Tue-Thu 9 AM | Founder |
| LinkedIn (company page) | 2×/week | Mon, Fri 11 AM | Studio lead |
| Site `/notes` (long-form) | 1×/month | First Monday | Founder |
| Twitter/X (founder) | 3×/week | 7 PM IST | Founder |
| Instagram (studio account) | 2×/week | 6 PM | Designer |
| Newsletter (Substack-style) | 1×/month | Last Friday | Founder |

## Content pillars — every post must fit one

1. **Build in public** — what we shipped this week, with timestamps
2. **Founder lessons** — opinions on running a studio (not generic advice)
3. **Case studies** — client wins, with permission and numbers
4. **Tooling deep-dives** — how we use Claude / Imagen / Vercel
5. **Hyderabad pulse** — local startup ecosystem commentary

Roughly: 30% Build in public · 25% Founder lessons · 20% Case studies · 15% Tooling · 10% Hyderabad.

## Post templates (copy-paste ready)

### A. The "build in public" post

```
This week at Brand Mint, Hyderabad:

→ [Specific output #1, with a number]
→ [Specific output #2, with a number]
→ [Specific output #3, with a number]

[One-line lesson from the week.]

Booking Q3 2026 — link in profile.
```

### B. The "founder lesson" post

```
[Specific situation that happened to you this week.]

[The thing you learned, in one paragraph.]

[The opposite belief — what most people in our space think.]

[Why your version is right, with one piece of evidence.]

— Brand Mint, Hyderabad
```

### C. The "case study" post

```
[Client name] came to us with [specific problem].

We shipped [specific deliverable] in [timeline].

Result: [single specific metric, ideally INR or %].

[One sentence about what made the difference.]

[Link to case study if public.]
```

### D. The "tooling deep-dive" post

```
A prompt that saved us 4 hours this week:

[Paste the actual prompt — be generous, share it.]

Why it works: [one sentence about the principle].

Used with [Claude Sonnet 4.6 / Gemini Flash / Imagen 4] at temp [X].
```

## Voice check before publishing

Every post must pass this 5-second check:

1. Has at least one specific number ✅/❌
2. Avoids the vocabulary blacklist ✅/❌
3. Sounds like a human, not a brand ✅/❌
4. Has one italic / emphasis word max ✅/❌
5. No emojis ✅/❌

If 4 of 5, ship. If 3 or fewer, rewrite.

## Repurposing flow

| Master | →    | Repurposes into |
|---|---|---|
| 1 long-form post (1500 words) | → | 3 LinkedIn carousels |
| 1 LinkedIn carousel | → | 1 Twitter thread |
| 1 Twitter thread | → | 3 Instagram quote tiles |
| 1 case study | → | 1 LinkedIn post + 1 newsletter feature + 1 site work-page entry |

This is how a 4-hour Monday writing session feeds 3 weeks of content.

## Tools

- **Buffer or Typefully** — schedule LinkedIn + Twitter
- **Substack or Beehiiv** — newsletter
- **Notion** — content calendar (this template)
- **Claude** — first-draft assist (with the voice agent system prompt from `08-AI-TOOLSTACK.md`)
- **Loom** — quick video clips for case study posts

## Metrics to track (monthly)

- LinkedIn impressions, engagement rate, follower growth
- Site `/notes` pageviews + email signups
- Inbound discovery calls *attributed to content* (ask in qualification)
- Newsletter open rate (target: 45%+)

If inbound from content is <20% of total inbound, the engine isn't compounding yet — keep at it for 90 days minimum before changing strategy.
