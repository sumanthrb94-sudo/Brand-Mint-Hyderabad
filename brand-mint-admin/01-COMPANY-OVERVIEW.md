# 01 · Company Overview

## Vision (10-year)

Be the studio that founder-led Indian companies hire when they need their brand and product to *compound* — when the design and engineering choices they make today need to still be paying back five years from now.

## Mission (3-year)

Ship 100 founder engagements that measurably moved revenue or product velocity. Become the default referral inside the Hyderabad / Bengaluru / Mumbai / Dubai founder grapevine for "I need a senior team that can do brand + site + tools + AI in one studio."

## Why now

Three structural shifts make this the right moment:

1. **AI raises the floor on craft.** A senior team augmented with Claude / Gemini / Imagen ships in days what took weeks. The bottleneck moves from execution to taste — which is exactly where Brand Mint plays.
2. **Indian founders are graduating past Fiverr-tier work.** Series A+ founders need agency-level quality but at INR pricing, not ₹50 L US-agency invoices. There is no premium-yet-INR studio category. We define it.
3. **Gulf money is looking south.** UAE / KSA founders are increasingly hiring Indian studios for work that 5 years ago they would have hired Dubai shops for. Hyderabad gives us a 2.5-hour flight to DXB and IST overlap.

## Market

### TAM (India + GCC, B2B creative + product services)

- India digital agency market: ~₹35,000 Cr (2026)
- Premium tier (₹5 L+ engagements): ~₹3,500 Cr
- GCC creative spend on Indian studios: ~₹1,200 Cr
- **Realistic addressable for a 5-person Hyderabad studio**: ₹15-25 Cr/year ceiling within 5 years

### Customer segments (priority order)

1. **Series A/B Indian SaaS** (₹10 Cr–₹100 Cr ARR). Need: marketing site that converts + internal tools to scale ops. Buying power: founder/CEO. Cycle: 2-4 weeks.
2. **D2C brands** at ₹5 Cr+ revenue. Need: brand refresh + Shopify/Razorpay storefront + content engine. Buying power: founder. Cycle: 1-3 weeks.
3. **Gulf real estate / hospitality / fashion**. Need: brand book + bilingual site + booking systems. Buying power: family-office principal. Cycle: 4-6 weeks.
4. **Indian PE-backed companies (₹100 Cr+)**. Need: rebrand + investor-grade site + ops dashboards. Buying power: CMO + CFO. Cycle: 6-12 weeks.

## Studio principles (the way we work)

1. **One brain per engagement.** A single senior owns the relationship end-to-end. No "account manager" → "creative director" → "engineer" telephone game.
2. **Live URL within two weeks.** Even if it's a placeholder. Real artifacts unblock decisions faster than decks.
3. **Numbers in every meeting.** No conversation ends without a metric to track and a date to revisit.
4. **The brand is a product.** A brand book is useless if it doesn't ship. Every guideline must be testable in code.
5. **Over-communicate; under-promise.** Daily Loom for the first 2 weeks of every engagement. Slack response SLA: 4 working hours.

## Studio location

HITEC City, Hyderabad. Co-working anchor (initially WeWork or similar). In-person kickoff hosted at the studio for every engagement. Travel-on-us to Bengaluru / Mumbai / Dubai for engagements above ₹5 L.

## Legal entity

Recommended: **Private Limited Company** (Pvt Ltd) — best for raising in future, separates personal liability, expected by Gulf clients. Alternative: **LLP** if you want lower compliance overhead and don't expect to raise. Sole proprietorship is *not* recommended once revenue crosses ₹40 L (GST registration anyway, plus signal/credibility).
