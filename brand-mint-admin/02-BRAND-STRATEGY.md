# 02 · Brand Strategy

## Positioning statement (the "north star sentence")

> For founder-led Indian and Gulf companies that need creative work to compound into measurable revenue, **Brand Mint** is the senior-led Hyderabad studio that ships brand systems, custom websites, internal tools and AI integrations in weeks not months — because every engagement is run by an 8+ year operator and priced in INR for founders who'd rather pay for taste than for a 40-slot agency overhead.

Memorise this. Every site headline, deck slide, and proposal line should be a derivative of it.

## Three-word identity

**Senior. Specific. Indian.**

| Word | What it commits us to |
|---|---|
| Senior | No interns billing client time. The pitcher builds. Voice/tone is operator-to-operator. |
| Specific | Numbers in every meeting. ICP narrowness. Refuse vague briefs. |
| Indian | INR pricing. Hyderabad ground. IST hours. Cultural context built in. |

When we make a decision, ask: does this make us *more* senior / specific / Indian, or less? More = ship it.

## Brand voice (5 principles, 5 words each)

1. Confident without arrogance.
2. Specific over abstract.
3. Warm but not buddy-buddy.
4. Editorial, never salesy.
5. Founder-to-founder always.

## Voice rubric — apply before any external copy ships

| Test | Pass example | Fail example |
|---|---|---|
| Has a number? | "+₹42.6 Cr in tracked revenue" | "Tons of impact" |
| Italicises one word? | "We mint *brands* that compound." | "We *truly truly* care" |
| Uses concrete nouns? | "HITEC City studio" | "Forward-thinking partners" |
| Avoids blacklist? | (see below) | (see below) |
| Reads aloud naturally? | Yes | "Synergistic holistic solution" |

## Vocabulary blacklist

Never ship: *innovative · solutions · cutting-edge · disruptive · synergy · holistic · leverage · paradigm · game-changer · world-class · best-in-class · next-gen · scalable · seamless · turnkey · end-to-end · craftsmanship · forward-thinking · ecosystem · transform · empower · unlock · reimagine.*

These words signal "agency that bills hourly," not "studio that ships."

## Italic emphasis policy

Use `<em>` to italicise **one** word per headline. Never two. The signature pattern is: *"We mint **brands** that compound."* — one foot stays grounded (sans), one foot lifts (italic).

## Numerals policy

Every quantitative claim — revenue, sessions, percentages, dates — set in **JetBrains Mono**. The visual handoff to monospace is the brand's "engineered" tell. If something is a number, it gets the mono treatment.

## ICP (ideal customer profile)

### Tier 1 — Bullseye

| Attribute | Profile |
|---|---|
| Stage | Series A or B Indian SaaS (₹10–100 Cr ARR) |
| Buyer | Founder/CEO directly |
| Trigger | About to raise / just raised; needs investor-grade brand and site |
| Engagement | Site (₹3-5 L) → Tools retainer (₹1.5 L/mo) |
| Decision time | 2-4 weeks |
| Where they are | LinkedIn, founder Slack groups, YC India network |

### Tier 2 — Strong fit

| Attribute | Profile |
|---|---|
| Stage | D2C brand at ₹5+ Cr revenue |
| Buyer | Founder, sometimes brand head |
| Trigger | Rebrand cycle; expanding to international |
| Engagement | Brand system (₹1.5-3 L) → Site (₹2-4 L) |

### Tier 3 — Acceptable

| Attribute | Profile |
|---|---|
| Region | UAE / KSA family-office or hospitality group |
| Buyer | Principal or COO |
| Trigger | Going digital-first; bilingual (EN/AR) needs |
| Engagement | Brand + site + ops console (₹8-15 L) |

## Anti-ICP — say no, fast

- Anyone asking for "just a logo"
- Anyone asking for "AI but cheap"
- Anyone whose budget is below ₹1 L
- Anyone whose CMO won't be on the call
- Anyone who can't articulate the metric they want moved

## Tone in different surfaces

- **Website** — Editorial confidence. Numbers up front. One italic emphasis per headline.
- **LinkedIn** — Founder-to-founder. Lead with a metric. Sign off "— Brand Mint, Hyderabad."
- **Proposals** — Boring is fine. Specific is mandatory. Every line item gets a deliverable + a date.
- **Client Slack** — Async-friendly. Loom-first. Daily updates first 2 weeks, weekly after.
- **Email** — Short subject lines. No "Hope you're well!" preamble. State the ask in line one.
